My Experience

Work, Mission, etc.

For the Strength of Youth (FSY)

From May 2024 – August 2024 ,I led 6 groups of youth, totaling 100+ youth over all sessions. I was stationed in the west coast area (Idaho, Utah, Washington, and California) working roughly 13 hours a day. I applied teambuilding skills to create a fun and inclusive environment, as well as leadership skills to teach effectively and provide direction for youth.

Mission Secretary Experience

As the General Secretary for the El Salvador San Salvador West/Belize Mission from February 2022 – September 2022, I kept inventories for all the church materials for the mission, reordering and distributing when necessary. I facilitated the mail for 120 missionaries, making sure that every missionary got their packages in an orderly fashion. I organized my Mission President’s schedule and created documents that outgoing missionaries needed. Coordinated welcome packets, bedding distribution and cell phones for all incoming missionaries.

As Technology Specialist, I oversaw 150 missionaries’ cell phones. This entailed ensuring that all phones had all the hardware/software installed and were working properly. I also coordinated with the Mission President to guarantee the proper technology was available for any meetings being held.
